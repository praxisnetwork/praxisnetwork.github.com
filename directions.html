Many of these programs are pilots or experiments with uncertain futures. Others anticipate longevity within their institution. Discover the ways that they plan for their future goals below.

Praxis Program CHI Initiative HOPE Mellon Scholars CUNY DHI UCLDH
Goal is dynamic sustainability, with continued contributions to the field as circumstances evolve over time. Hopes to refine based on each year's successes and challenges to create a sustainable and rigorous program. Hopes to receive an increasing number of applications to make the program more selective. Major goal is to see more faculty invested. Very much an experiment, and they are learning as they go. One hope is to retain some students in subsequent years to build up a base of knowledge and expertise. Hopes to expand collaboration to include more faculty and students. Sustained enrollment is a concern, as funding and fee structures at UK universities are changing in a way that is difficult for students.
